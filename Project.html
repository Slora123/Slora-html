A Project on Culture of India

Unheard Story of India

My team members and I made a video for our project based on an unheard story of India
that beautifully showcases its rich culture. I collected the necessary materials and
edited the video to ensure it effectively conveys the story. Through this project,
we aim to highlight a lesser-known yet significant part of India's heritage and bring
its cultural importance to a wider audience.
